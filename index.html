BLS benchmark
BLS JOLTS separation rates · monthly (Dec 2025, seasonally adjusted) & annualized
Voluntary = quits · Involuntary = layoffs & discharges. Annual ≈ monthly rate × 12. WS Display reflects this year's average monthly pace for the selected view, annualized — for a partial year it's the projected full-year pace. Green = below benchmark, red = above. Source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Job Openings and Labor Turnover Survey.
